PROFILE

“BOOKSHIP.COM” is one of the world's largest integrated international container transportation and logistics companies. As one of Singapore's most recognized global brands, We provide customers with fully-integrated logistics and containerized (General Purpose, Food Grade and Flexibag Load Container) transportation services, with a network that encompasses Asia, India and Indonesia Outports, like Pekan Baru (Sungai Dukuh/Perawang), Padang, Palembang, Bangka, Belitung, Bengkulu, Sibolga, Tanjung Pinang, Dumai, Pontianak,Kumai, Sampit, Banjamarsin, Samarinda, Tarakan, Balikpapan, Berau, Batulicin,

Benoa/Bali, Makasar, Palu (Pantoloan), Kendari, Bitung (Manado), Gorontalo, Bau Bau, Ambon,Ternate, Tual, Kupang, Maumere, Wini, Ampenan, Fak Fak, Manokwari, Sorong, Jayapura, Merauke, Nabire, Timika, Biak, Serui, Kaimana, Dili, Tangkiang, Bintan Islang/Kijang Port and Batam.


We are well respected in the industry with a reputation for providing customer-focused solutions, quality services and continual innovation. “BookShip.com” is one of the leading international carriers serving China, providing a full range of logistics and transportation services throughout the country. It is also an industry leader in the use of information technology and e-commerce to manage the entire cargo process.
